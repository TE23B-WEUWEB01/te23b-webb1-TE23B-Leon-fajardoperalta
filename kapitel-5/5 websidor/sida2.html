Rakim Athelaston Mayers[2] (born October 3, 1988), known professionally as A$AP Rocky (/ˈeɪsæp/ AY-sap; stylized as A$AP Rocky), is an American-Barbadian rapper.[3] Born and raised in Harlem, he embarked on his musical career as a member of the hip hop collective ASAP Mob, from which he adopted his moniker. In August 2011, Mayers' single "Peso" was leaked online, and within weeks, began receiving radio airplay.[4] He signed with Polo Grounds Music, an imprint of RCA Records in October of that year, and shortly after, released his debut mixtape, Live. Love. A$AP (2011) to widespread critical acclaim.[5][6]
